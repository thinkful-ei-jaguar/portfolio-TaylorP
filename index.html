MyBookmarks

My Bookmarks is a third-party(separate of the browser) application that stores your favorite websites in one easy to use, and easy to read, environment. You can store all your websites in one place no matter what browser you want to use and when. I built this for the freedom of moving to a new browser or when you get a new computer, you don't have to lose all your saved website.
